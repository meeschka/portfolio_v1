About Me

I went into geological engineering because I loved solving problems and learning new things. After spending some time in industry, I realized that I wasn't fulfilling those two goals. I've pivoted into web development because I still love working as part of a team to solve complex technical challenges, but also love the thrill that comes from overcoming a complex challenge, implementing a new feature, or finally getting the code to run just right. If you are looking for someone who is detail-oriented, driven, and excited to learn, don't hesitate to get in touch!
