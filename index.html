About Me

I went into geological engineering because I loved solving problems and learning new things. After spending some time in industry, I realized that I wasn't fulfilling those two goals. I've pivoted into web development because I still love working as part of a team to solve complex technical challenges, but also love the thrill that comes from overcoming a challenging problem, implementing a new feature, or finally getting the code to run just right. I combine the problem-solving skills and analytical thinking of a professional engineer with a strong base of software design fundamentals learned in an intensive training bootcamp. If you are looking for someone who is detail-oriented, driven, and excited to learn, don't hesitate to get in touch!

Portfolio

Recime

A full-stack app designed and built by me using MongoDB, Express, and Node. This recipe app allows users to save and share recipes, as well as 'fork' recipes by others to make changes based on their own preferences.

This full-stack app was built as part of the General Assembly Software Engineering Immersive Curriculum. It was designed and built entirely by me.
Jobbly

A full-stack Django app using Postgres to track job application status and interviews. This application was developed by a three-person team using agile workflow. As the primary back-end engineer, I implemented the job search API, calendar plugin, and back-end logic throughout much of the site, and deployed the app to Heroku .
Checkpoint

A MERN-stack app created and built by me as part of the General Assembly Software Engineering Immersive Course. Checkpoint acts as a framework for users to set and manage long-term goals and a way of tracking progress to stay motivated. Users can opt-in to weekly text reminders to stay focused on their objectives.
